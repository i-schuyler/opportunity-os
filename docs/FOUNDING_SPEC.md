# FOUNDING_SPEC.md

## Product name
Opportunity OS

## Positioning
Opportunity OS is a calm personal tool for opportunity seekers. It helps individuals track opportunities, manage follow-ups, and receive clear next-step guidance.

## Revenue posture
- Free tier
- Paid tier: $9/month
- Founder lifetime: $79 one-time, limited to the first 50 customers

## Launch message
Broad audience: opportunity seekers generally.
Lead example: housing and barter opportunities.

## v0.1 scope
- Authentication
- Opportunity CRUD
- Templates
- AI reply drafting
- Weekly AI summary
- Billing gate
- Landing page

## Non-goals
- Marketplace
- Social feed
- Native mobile app
- High-autonomy external actions without user review
- Complex third-party integrations

## Public-readable stance
This repository may be publicly visible for transparency and story value, but it is not intended to grant open-source reuse rights unless a license is explicitly added later.
